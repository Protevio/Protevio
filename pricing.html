Frequently asked questions

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes, cancel anytime from your dashboard. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.
What payment methods do you accept?
Visa, Mastercard, Amex, SEPA, iDEAL, Bancontact, and more through our secure payment processor.
Can I switch between plans?
Yes. Upgrade anytime (prorated). Downgrade takes effect at the next billing cycle.
What is a Face Alert?
Face Alerts monitor our database for new appearances of a face. You get notified when new matches are found — great for tracking unauthorized photo use.
Is my data safe?
Uploaded photos are processed temporarily and not stored permanently. We use encryption in transit and at rest.
