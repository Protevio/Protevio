How Our Face Recognition Technology Works

Protevio uses a multi-stage pipeline built on state-of-the-art computer vision research. When you upload a photo, our system first detects all faces using a neural network trained on extensive datasets. Each detected face is processed through a deep embedding network that converts it into a 512-dimensional mathematical representation — essentially a unique facial fingerprint.

This embedding captures the essential geometry and features of the face in a way that is invariant to lighting, angle, expression, and even aging. The embedding is compared against our database using HNSW (Hierarchical Navigable Small World) vector indexing, enabling sub-second searches across our entire index.

The Technical Pipeline

What happens behind the scenes when you search a face.

1

Face Detection & Alignment

Our neural network (InsightFace RetinaFace) detects all faces in your photo, estimates landmarks, and aligns each face to a canonical 112×112 crop for optimal embedding quality.

2

Embedding Extraction

Each aligned face passes through a deep ArcFace network (buffalo_l) that outputs a 512-dimensional L2-normalized vector — a mathematical fingerprint of the face’s unique geometry.

3

HNSW Nearest Neighbor Search

The embedding is compared against our PostgreSQL pgvector index using cosine similarity. HNSW graph traversal finds the top matches across the entire database in milliseconds.

Face Recognition Technology FAQ

What is a face recognition search engine?
It uses AI to match faces in photos rather than keywords or pixel patterns. Upload a photo containing a face, and the engine finds all matching faces across its indexed database, returning the websites and images where that face appears.
How is facial recognition different from reverse image search?
Reverse image search matches pixel patterns and image similarity. Facial recognition analyzes facial features — bone structure, eye spacing, facial geometry — meaning it matches faces across completely different photos, angles, and lighting conditions.
What specific technology does Protevio use?
InsightFace (buffalo_l model) for face detection and embedding extraction, PostgreSQL with pgvector for vector storage, HNSW indexing for fast approximate nearest neighbor search, and MiVOLO for AI-powered age estimation and minor protection.
Is facial recognition search safe to use?
Protevio is designed with privacy at its core. Uploaded photos are never stored or used for training. We comply with GDPR and offer opt-out for anyone who wants their face removed. Minor protection systems automatically block searches involving children.
Can I upload multiple photos for better accuracy?
Yes. Upload up to 5 photos of the same face. Protevio averages the embeddings to create a noise-reduced representation that finds more matches than any single photo alone.
